Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29/10/2024

Your security is important to us. For this reason, the personal data you share with us is carefully protected.

As the Site data controller, this privacy and personal data protection policy aims to inform you about which personal data will be processed for which purposes, with whom and why the processed data can be shared, our data processing methods and legal grounds, and your rights regarding the processed data.

Personal Data Collected, Collection Methods, and Legal Grounds

Your IP address and user agent information will be processed through automated or non-automated methods and sometimes obtained from third parties such as analytics providers, advertising networks, search data providers, and technology providers via cookies and other technologies, recorded, stored, and updated in the context of our service and contractual relationship and will be processed based on the legitimate interest processing condition.

Purpose of Processing Your Personal Data

The personal data you share with us will only be processed in order to analyze and ensure that the services we offer are provided in the best possible way, to allow access to these services and maximize their benefits, to improve our services based on your needs, to introduce you to broader service providers within legal frameworks, and to fulfill legal obligations (such as sharing personal data with judicial and administrative authorities when requested), and will be processed and updated in a purpose-appropriate and proportionate manner during the contract and service period.

Who Your Personal Data May Be Shared With and For What Purposes

The personal data you share with us may be shared with third parties, institutions, and organizations we work with in Turkey and abroad, with whom we have a contractual relationship, and with judicial and administrative authorities when requested, under the condition that necessary technical and administrative measures are taken.

Your Rights as a Person Whose Personal Data Is Processed

According to Article 11 of the Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK), everyone can exercise the following rights by applying to the data controller:

  • Learn whether personal data is processed,
  • Request information about personal data if it has been processed,
  • Learn the purpose of processing personal data and whether they are used in accordance with their purpose,
  • Know the third parties to whom personal data is transferred, whether inside or outside the country,
  • Request correction if personal data is processed incompletely or inaccurately,
  • Request deletion or destruction of personal data,
  • Request that the actions taken under (e) and (f) be communicated to third parties to whom personal data has been transferred,
  • Object to the analysis of personal data exclusively through automated systems, which results in a negative consequence for the person,
  • Request compensation for damages in case of damage due to unlawful processing of personal data.
